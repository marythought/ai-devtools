The Spoon Theory

Coined by Christine Miserandino to explain her experience living with a chronic illness, "Spoon Theory" is a metaphor used to describe the amount of mental or physical energy a person has available for daily activities. Each spoon represents a unit of energy, and when you're out of spoons, you're out of energy.

In the context of a TODO app, it's a reminder that:

  • Every task requires physical, mental, and/or emotional energy (spoons)
  • We all have limited resources each day
  • It's important to prioritize what matters most
  • Taking care of yourself is essential
